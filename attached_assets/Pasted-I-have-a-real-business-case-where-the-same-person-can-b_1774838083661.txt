I have a real business case where the same person can be 
both a customer (buys from me) AND a supplier (I buy from them).

Current system problem:
- customers table: tracks people who buy from me
- suppliers table: tracks people I buy from
- They are completely separate — no link between them
- This causes wrong account statements when same person 
  appears in both tables

## Required Feature: Linked Customer-Supplier Account

### 1. Database Changes:
Add a column to both tables:
- customers: linked_supplier_id (nullable, foreign key → suppliers.id)
- suppliers: linked_customer_id (nullable, foreign key → customers.id)

This creates a bidirectional link between the same person
in both tables.

### 2. UI Changes — Customer Form:
When adding/editing a customer, add:
- Toggle: "هل يبيع لك أيضاً؟" (Is this also a supplier?)
- If YES: show supplier selector dropdown
  "اربط بمورد موجود" OR "إنشاء مورد جديد تلقائياً"
- If creating new: auto-fill supplier name from customer name

Same toggle on Supplier form:
- "هل يشتري منك أيضاً؟"
- Link to existing customer or create new

### 3. Combined Account Statement:
Create GET /api/contacts/:id/full-statement
Where contact can be customer_id or supplier_id.

This endpoint must return ALL transactions merged and 
sorted by date:

| التاريخ | النوع | البيان | مدين (له) | دائن (عليه) | الرصيد |
|---------|-------|--------|-----------|-------------|--------|

Transaction types to include:
- مبيعات له (sale to customer) → دائن (he owes us)
- مشتريات منه (purchase from supplier) → مدين (we owe him)
- سند قبض (receipt voucher) → يقلل ما عليه
- سند صرف (payment voucher) → يقلل ما نحن مدينون به
- مرتجع مبيعات → عكس المبيعات
- مرتجع مشتريات → عكس المشتريات
- رصيد أول المدة → يظهر أول سطر

Running balance calculation:
+ sales amount (customer owes us)
- purchases amount (we owe supplier)
+ receipts (reduces what customer owes)
- payments (reduces what we owe supplier)

Final balance:
- Positive = customer owes US money
- Negative = WE owe supplier money
- Show clearly: "له علينا 4,000 ج.م" or "عليه لنا 1,000 ج.م"

### 4. Purchases Page Fix:
In the purchase invoice form:
- Change party selector to search BOTH customers and suppliers
- Label: "المورد / الطرف الآخر"
- Search returns: all suppliers + all customers who are linked 
  to a supplier
- When a linked customer is selected:
  → automatically use their linked supplier_id for the purchase
  → show note: "هذا العميل مرتبط بحساب مورد"

### 5. Statement Page UI:
Create a new "كشف الحساب الموحد" view accessible from:
- Customer detail page → button "كشف الحساب الكامل"
- Supplier detail page → same button

The statement shows:
- Contact info header (name, phone, type: عميل/مورد/كلاهما)
- Balance summary box:
  • إجمالي المبيعات له: xx,xxx
  • إجمالي المشتريات منه: xx,xxx  
  • صافي الرصيد: xx,xxx (colored: green=he owes us, red=we owe him)
- Full transactions table (all types merged, sorted by date)
- Filter by date range
- Export to PDF button

### 6. Validation Rules:
- Cannot link a customer to themselves as supplier
- Cannot create circular links
- When deleting a customer who is linked to supplier:
  → warn: "هذا العميل مرتبط بمورد، هل تريد حذف الارتباط أيضاً؟"
