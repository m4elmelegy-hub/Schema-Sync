3) REMOVE ROOT CAUSE OF BREAKAGE
================================

Search the project and eliminate theme-breaking patterns:

❌ inline background colors
❌ inline rgba() UI colors
❌ hardcoded dark-only backgrounds
❌ text-white/xx in light mode
❌ bg-[#111827] / bg-[#0f172a] directly inside pages
❌ random component-specific styling

Replace all of them with theme classes/tokens.

==================================================
4) LIGHT MODE QUALITY BAR
=========================

Light mode must be first-class, not patched.

Rules:

* white or soft neutral surfaces
* dark readable text
* no faded gray numbers
* no low opacity important text
* borders visible and elegant
* dropdowns and modals must match the system

Typography:

* body: 16px
* labels: 14–15px / 600
* section titles: 18–20px
* important numbers: 22px+

==================================================
5) DARK MODE QUALITY BAR
========================

Dark mode must be elegant, not heavy.

Rules:

* deep but readable background
* reduced glow
* clear borders
* avoid gaming-style contrast
* numbers and actions remain clear

==================================================

=================================

Standardize all:

* inputs
