11) Important accounting automation rules
Implement these mappings:
- Cash sale:
  Debit treasury/bank
  Credit sales revenue
  Credit VAT output if tax exists
  Debit COGS
  Credit inventory
- Credit sale:
  Debit customer receivable
  Credit sales revenue
  Credit VAT output if tax exists
  Debit COGS
  Credit inventory
- Cash purchase:
  Debit inventory
  Debit VAT input if applicable
  Credit treasury/bank
- Credit purchase:
  Debit inventory
  Debit VAT input if applicable
  Credit supplier payable
- Customer payment:
  Debit treasury/bank
  Credit customer receivable
- Supplier payment:
  Debit supplier payable
  Credit treasury/bank
- Expense payment:
  Debit expense account
  Credit treasury/bank
- Treasury transfer:
  Debit destination treasury
  Credit source treasury
- Inventory increase adjustment:
  Debit inventory
  Credit inventory adjustment gain or suspense account
- Inventory decrease adjustment:
  Debit inventory adjustment loss
  Credit inventory
